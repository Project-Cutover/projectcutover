When to engage Project Cutover

A platform migration is approaching go-live, multiple teams and vendors are involved, and the activation window requires structured coordination, clear escalation paths, and controlled execution.

What Project Cutover Does

Project Cutover provides independent leadership for complex SaaS migrations and high-stakes system activations. Engagements focus on cutover planning, cross-team deployment coordination, command center operations, and real-time issue escalation during production go-live windows.

Services

Typical engagement areas include:

  • Cutover planning and execution
  • Go-live command center leadership
  • Cross-team deployment coordination
  • Incident and escalation management
  • Platform migration oversight

Operating Approach

  • Clear readiness gates and go / no-go decision criteria
  • Structured cutover runbooks with sequencing and rollback plans
  • Command center coordination across engineering, operations, and vendors
  • Real-time issue triage and escalation management
  • Post-launch stabilization and operational handoff

Representative Experience

Project Cutover leadership draws on enterprise deployment experience including multi-property hospitality platform migrations involving reservation systems, OTA distribution integrations, and coordinated vendor cutovers across engineering, operations, and external partners.

Engagement

Typical engagements range from 4–12 weeks depending on migration scope, deployment complexity, and stabilization requirements.
